Our Journey

From Frontier to Modern Table

Founded in 1972, Copper Chimney was born out of a passion for the legendary frontier cuisine. Our journey began with the simple idea of bringing the robust flavors of North India to the global stage, using copper tandoors and hand-picked spices.

Today, we stand as a beacon of authentic Indian heritage, where every slow-cooked curry and smoke-infused kebab is a tribute to the master chefs who came before us. We blend rustic traditions with modern elegance to create an experience that is truly timeless.
